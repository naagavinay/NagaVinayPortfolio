CUSTOMER CHURN ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

Designed and developed a customer churn analysis dashboard. In this project, I embarked on a comprehensive exploration of customer behavior to better understand the factors influencing churn. My approach involved a thorough comparison between active and churned customer profiles, uncovering nuanced distinctions that provided critical insights. By closely examining key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Average Monthly Charge and Average Total Charge, I gained a deeper understanding of the financial aspects driving customer decisions. Additionally, I took a granular approach to churn prediction, dissecting customer demographics, connection types, age groups, gender, and service preferences to identify specific segments more likely to churn. This intelligence equips businesses with the knowledge needed to implement targeted retention strategies, ultimately enhancing customer loyalty and bolstering overall performance.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

Designed and developed a Project Management Analysis dashboard. In this project, I spearheaded the development of a comprehensive Project Management Dashboard, equipped with a range of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) such as Total Income Generation, Process Improvement, Cost Reduction, and Working Capital. To simplify complex data, I utilized a decomposition tree, providing an intuitive visual representation of completion rates across different project phases and departments. This dashboard boasts high interactivity, featuring a diverse set of visuals like column charts, funnels, pie charts, cards, gauge charts, slicers, and filters for years and project categories (In-Progress, On-Hold, Completed, and Cancelled). Executives can swiftly make informed decisions regarding project managers, specific project phases, and budget allocation, leveraging the user-friendly interface and data-rich visuals at their disposal.
